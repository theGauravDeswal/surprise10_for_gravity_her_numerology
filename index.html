Some people are understood in conversations…

Some… require silence to be heard.

This is not just a reading.

This is you… seen.

Jayanti

(Isha… the one who returns to softness)

You were never ‘too much’…

You were just deeply alive.

You feel everything… intensely.

You react… because you care.

You speak… because silence was never your nature.

People called it dramatic…

But it was always… real.

There is a girl in you…

Who still loves flowers… pink… magic…

Who still wants someone to go crazy for her…

She didn’t disappear.

She just learned to settle for less.

You fear losing control…

You fear… not being held when it matters.

But what you really fear is…

giving your heart fully… and not being met there.

But here’s the truth about you…

You don’t break.

You adapt.

You feel… and still move forward.

That’s not weakness.

That’s rare.

You don’t fall for people…

You fall for how they make you feel.

Seen.

Chosen.

Desired… without asking.

And when you finally meet someone…

Who listens beyond your words…

Something in you softens again.

You don’t need to become anything more…

The right person doesn’t fix your chaos…

He chooses you in it.

Stay the way you are…

The ones meant for you…

won’t ask you to be less.
